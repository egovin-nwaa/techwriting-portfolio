# HOW TO OPEN A MEXC ACCOUNT

MEXC is a global cryptocurrency exchange where you can buy, sell, and trade 
digital assets like Bitcoin and Ethereum. They offer a range of services for 
trading spots, perpetual futures, and many others. 
This guide will show you how to sign up and create your own MEXC account.

---

## STEP 1: VISIT THE GOOGLE PLAY STORE OR APPLE APP STORE
- Visit any of the stores available on your mobile phone and search for ***MEXC***. The correct app should be listed as ***MEXC: Buy Bitcoin BTC & 
Crypto***.
- Click on ***Install***.
- After Installation, open the app and click on ***Sign Up***.

## STEP 2: ENTER YOUR DETAILS
- You can sign up using your email or mobile number.
- Create a strong password that consists of letters, numbers, and symbols.
- Agree to the terms and click ***Sign up***.

## STEP 3: VERIFY YOUR ACCOUNT
- You will receive a verification code from MEXC in your email or phone.
- Input the code and confirm your registration.


Once you are verified, you can deposit crypto or buy using your local currency 
and start trading.
